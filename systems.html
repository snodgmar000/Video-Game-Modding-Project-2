System Homebrew

System Homebrew is a way to get video game mods on console. But there is a issue, the companys behind the system don't want you to homebrew your system

Most older systems are easy to homebrew like the Wii and 3DS and have step by step guides online

But what can you do with a homebrewed system?

  • Use community made mods for the games on the system
  • Use 3rd-party community servers for online games (For example: Pretendo restores the Wii U online functionality)
  • Play community made games that are not licensed by the company behind the system
  • Theres a lot more you can do but that is a few legal things you can do with a modded system
